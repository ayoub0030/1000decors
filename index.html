It all begins here, with the perfect tree. Each trunk is chosen for its strength, grain pattern, and sustainability. This careful selection is where the journey starts, laying the foundation for every handcrafted piece that follows.

From the raw trunk, our artisan's hands bring each piece of wood to life. With precise tools and a deep respect for Berber Amazigh heritage, we carve, shape, and assemble every element—blending time-honored techniques with modern craftsmanship.

Every curve and motif finds its place. Polished and refined, the wood transforms into a statement of both function and art—embodying the soul of the tree and the spirit of our craft in one harmonious design.
